Returns & Refund Policy

Last Updated: 02.01.2026

1. General Policy

At The Curated Vintage Jewelry, each item is carefully selected and often one of a kind. Due to the nature of vintage jewelry and our curated business model, all purchases are handled individually and with particular attention to detail.

For this reason, all sales are considered final, except as expressly stated in this policy. We encourage customers to review product descriptions, measurements, and images carefully and to contact us with any questions prior to placing an order.

2. Damaged Items Upon Arrival

A full refund to the original payment method is issued only if an item arrives damaged during shipping.

To report a damaged item, you must:

  • Notify us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide your order number
  • Provide clear photographic evidence of the damage and original packaging

Once the claim is reviewed and approved, we will provide further instructions regarding the return of the item (if applicable) and process the refund in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws.

Claims submitted outside the 48-hour window may not be accepted.

3. All Other Return Requests

For all other situations, including but not limited to:

  • Change of mind
  • Personal preference
  • Sizing or fit concerns
  • Styling expectations
  • Gifting decisions
  • Normal signs of vintage wear described in the product listing

No refunds to the original payment method will be issued.

In approved cases, we offer store credit only, in accordance with the conditions outlined below.

4. Store Credit Conditions

Store credit issued by The Curated Vintage Jewelry:

  • Is equal to the original purchase value of the item
  • Is valid for 24 months from the date of issue
  • Is non-transferable
  • Is not redeemable for cash
  • Can be used toward future purchases only

Store credit allows flexibility for our customers while preserving the integrity of our limited, curated inventory.

5. Return Conditions

Any approved return must meet the following conditions:

  • The item must be unworn and unused
  • The item must be in the same condition as received
  • The item must be returned following our written instructions

Items showing signs of wear, alteration, damage after delivery, or handling inconsistent with normal inspection will not be accepted.

6. Vintage Jewelry Disclaimer

All products sold by The Curated Vintage Jewelry are vintage or curated items. Minor imperfections, patina, or signs of age are inherent to vintage pieces and are not considered defects.

By placing an order, you acknowledge and accept the nature of vintage jewelry.
