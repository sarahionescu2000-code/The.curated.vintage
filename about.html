1. About The Curated Vintage Jewelry

The Curated Vintage Jewelry is an online boutique specializing in carefully selected vintage and curated jewelry. Each item is chosen individually for its character, craftsmanship, and timeless appeal.

We operate on a small-batch, curated model. Most items available in our store are one of a kind or available in extremely limited quantities. Once sold, items are unlikely to be restocked.

Due to the nature of vintage jewelry, minor signs of age, wear, or patina may be present. These characteristics are part of the item’s history and uniqueness and are not considered defects.

2. Nature of Products

All products sold by The Curated Vintage Jewelry are vintage or curated items. As such, they may differ from modern, mass-produced jewelry.

  • Variations in color, texture, or finish may occur
  • Measurements are provided to the best of our ability but may vary slightly
  • Screen settings may affect color representation

By placing an order, you acknowledge and accept the nature of vintage products.

3. Returns, Refunds & Store Credit Policy

Due to the nature of vintage jewelry and our curated business model, all sales are considered final, subject to the conditions outlined below.

3.1 Damaged Items Upon Arrival (Cash Refund)

A full refund to the original payment method is issued only if an item arrives damaged during shipping.

To qualify for a refund due to damage, you must:

  • Notify us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide your order number
  • Provide clear photos of the damaged item and original packaging

Once the claim is reviewed and approved, we will provide return instructions (if applicable) and process the refund accordingly.

3.2 All Other Return Requests (Store Credit Only)

For all other situations, including but not limited to:

  • Change of mind
  • Personal preference
  • Sizing or fit concerns
  • Styling expectations
  • Gifting decisions

We do not issue refunds to the original payment method. Approved cases are compensated exclusively with store credit.

3.3 Store Credit Conditions

  • Store credit equals the value of the returned item
  • Valid for 24 months from the date of issue
  • Non-transferable
  • Not redeemable for cash
  • Usable for future purchases only

3.4 Return Conditions

Any approved return must meet the following conditions:

  • Item must be unworn and unused
  • Item must be in the same condition as received
  • Item must be returned following our instructions

Items showing signs of wear, alteration, or damage after delivery will not be accepted.
