Returns & Store Credit Policy

At The Curated Vintage Jewelry, each piece is carefully curated and often one of a kind. For this reason, all sales are considered final.

Final Sale

We do not accept returns for reasons such as change of mind, personal preference, sizing, or styling concerns.

Damaged Items Upon Arrival

Returns are accepted only if the item arrives damaged during shipping. If this occurs, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery and include:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos of the item and packaging

Store Credit

We do not issue refunds to the original payment method. Approved cases are compensated exclusively with store credit.

Store credit:

  • Is equal to the value of the returned item
  • Is valid for 24 months from the issue date
  • Cannot be exchanged for cash
