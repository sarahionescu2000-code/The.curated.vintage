Returns & Store Credit Policy

At The Curated Vintage Jewelry, each piece is carefully curated and often one of a kind. For this reason, all sales are considered final.

Final Sale

We do not accept returns for reasons such as change of mind, personal preference, sizing, or styling concerns.

Damaged Items Upon Arrival

Returns are accepted only if the item arrives damaged during shipping. If this happens, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery and include:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos of the item and original packaging

Store Credit (No Cash Refunds)

We do not issue refunds to the original payment method. Approved cases are compensated exclusively with store credit.

  • Store credit equals the value of the returned item
  • Valid for 24 months from the issue date
  • Non-transferable and non-redeemable for cash
  • Usable on future purchases only

Vintage Jewelry Disclaimer

All items sold are vintage. Minor signs of age, wear, or patina are part of the item’s character and are not considered defects.
