About The Curated Vintage

The Curated Vintage Jewelry is an online boutique offering carefully selected vintage jewelry. Each piece is chosen for its character, wearability, and timeless appeal.

We focus on small, intentional selections rather than mass-produced collections. All items are vintage and may show gentle signs of age that add to their individuality.

Returns & Store Credit Policy

Due to the nature of vintage jewelry, all sales are final. We do not accept returns for change of mind, personal preference, sizing, or styling concerns.

Damaged Items Upon Arrival

Returns are accepted only if an item arrives damaged during shipping. In such cases, you must contact us within 48 hours of delivery and provide:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos of the item and original packaging

Store Credit

We do not issue refunds to the original payment method. Approved claims are compensated exclusively with store credit.

  • Store credit equals the value of the item
  • Valid for 24 months from the date of issue
  • Non-transferable and not redeemable for cash
  • Usable for future purchases only

Minor signs of age, wear, or patina are normal for vintage items and are not considered defects.
